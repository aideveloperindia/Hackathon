# Hall Ticket Number Patterns

## CSM Branch Pattern

**Format:** 22271A66XX (where XX = 00-60)

- **Range:** 22271A6600 to 22271A6660
- **Total:** 60 students
- **Pattern:** Sequential numeric from 00 to 60

**Examples:**
- 22271A6600
- 22271A6601
- 22271A6602
- ...
- 22271A6660

## CSE Branch Pattern

### Pattern 1: Standard Numeric Range
**Format:** 22271A05XX (where XX = 00-99)

- **Range:** 22271A0500 to 22271A0599
- **Total:** 100 students
- **Pattern:** Sequential numeric from 00 to 99

**Examples:**
- 22271A0500
- 22271A0501
- ...
- 22271A0599

### Pattern 2: Letter A Series
**Format:** 22271A05AX (where X = 1-9)

- **Range:** 22271A05A1 to 22271A05A9
- **Total:** 9 students
- **Pattern:** Letter 'A' followed by digit 1-9

**Examples:**
- 22271A05A1
- 22271A05A2
- ...
- 22271A05A9

### Pattern 3: Letter B Series
**Format:** 22271A05BX (where X = 1-9)

- **Range:** 22271A05B1 to 22271A05B9
- **Total:** 9 students
- **Pattern:** Letter 'B' followed by digit 1-9

**Examples:**
- 22271A05B1
- 22271A05B2
- ...
- 22271A05B9

### Pattern 4: Letter C Series
**Format:** 22271A05CX (where X = 1-9)

- **Range:** 22271A05C1 to 22271A05C9
- **Total:** 9 students
- **Pattern:** Letter 'C' followed by digit 1-9

**Examples:**
- 22271A05C1
- 22271A05C2
- ...
- 22271A05C9

## Notes

- All HT numbers are case-sensitive
- The letter in CSE patterns (A, B, C) is uppercase
- Registration validation checks exact HT number match
- Each HT number can only be used once for registration
